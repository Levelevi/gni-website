About GNI

Fifteen years of academic excellence, community trust, and student success in the heart of Punjab.

Who We Are

Built on Values. Driven by Excellence.

Guru Nanak Institute of Technology was founded with a singular purpose — to make quality higher education accessible to the students of Punjab. Inspired by the teachings of Guru Nanak Dev Ji, we believe in equality, service, and the power of knowledge to transform lives. Today, GNI is a trusted name in Ludhiana, known for its academic standards, dedicated faculty, and student-first approach.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality education that empowers students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to excel in their chosen careers and contribute meaningfully to society.

🌟

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in Punjab recognized for academic excellence, innovation in teaching, and the holistic development of students — producing graduates who are industry-ready, ethical, and socially responsible.

Our Journey

A Legacy of Learning

From humble beginnings to one of Ludhiana's trusted institutes — our story.

2009
GNI Founded
Guru Nanak Institute of Technology was established on GT Road, Ludhiana with BCA and B.Com as the founding programs. The institute started with 120 students and a vision to serve Punjab.
2012
PTU Affiliation Granted
GNI received full affiliation from Punjab Technical University, validating its academic standards and opening doors for students to earn nationally recognized degrees.
2015
BBA & MCA Programs Launched
Responding to growing industry demand, GNI expanded its academic portfolio with BBA and MCA programs, welcoming a new generation of management and tech students.
2018
New Campus Infrastructure
Major expansion of campus facilities — new computer labs, a digital library, seminar halls, and a dedicated student activity center were inaugurated.
2021
Online Learning Integration
GNI successfully integrated digital learning tools and hybrid teaching models, ensuring uninterrupted education and expanding access to students across Punjab.
2024
2500+ Students & Growing
Today, GNI is home to over 2500 students across six programs, with a strong alumni network and continued commitment to excellence in education.

Campus Life

World-Class Infrastructure

Modern facilities designed to support learning, research, and personal growth.

💻
Computer Labs
5 fully equipped computer labs with 200+ systems, high-speed internet, and the latest software tools for programming and development.
📖
Digital Library
A rich collection of 10,000+ books, journals, and digital resources. Subscribed to national e-library portals for research access.
🏛️
Seminar Halls
Three air-conditioned seminar halls with modern AV equipment, used for guest lectures, workshops, and student presentations.
🍽️
Cafeteria
A hygienic, subsidized canteen serving nutritious meals and snacks throughout the day, providing a social hub for students.
🏸
Sports Facilities
Outdoor grounds for cricket and football, indoor facilities for badminton and table tennis, and an annual inter-college sports meet.

Transport
GNI operates a fleet of buses covering major routes across Ludhiana and surrounding areas, ensuring safe and convenient daily commute.
