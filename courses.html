How to Join

Admission Process

Four simple steps to secure your seat at GNI.

1
Apply Online
Fill out the application form on our website or visit the admissions office to collect a physical form.
2
Document Verification
Submit your mark sheets, ID proof, and photographs. Our team verifies eligibility as per PTU norms.
3
Merit List
A merit list is published based on qualifying exam scores. Shortlisted candidates are notified by SMS and email.
4
Enrollment
Pay the semester fee, collect your student ID, and you are officially a GNI student. Welcome aboard!
